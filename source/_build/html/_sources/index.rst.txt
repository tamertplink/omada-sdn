TP-Link Omada SDN Cookbook
==========================

.. warning::

   This is a community host work in progress document.
   Please go to tp-link.com for product information and official guides.

TP-Link Omada SDN is an easy-to-use business class corporate network system. Empowered by the Omada controller and TP-link cloud services, a business network system can be built with less effort. A general electrician can help with physical installation without the knowledge of your network design. And, you can deploy, set up and manage multiple networks in multiple locations without leaving your control room. The Omada SDN solution including three flavors of controller, hardware, software, and cloud-based controller, Omada EAP wireless access points, JetStream managed business switches, and SafeStream business routers.


About this cookbook
-------------------

This is a community driven cookbook project. This cookbook was designed for hands-on system design and management of the TP-Link Omada SDN system. It records the system set up steps and design considerations to specific use cases.  You can contribute your ideas and your way to use the system as well. Product information from TP-Link or any other company were gathered from the public records for reference purpose. Please visit the `TP-Link web site`_ for TP-Link product or respective vendor website. 

.. _TP-Link web site: https://www.tp-link.com/us/omada-sdn/

This document was designed for the community. Contributions and comments are welcomed through GitHub.
